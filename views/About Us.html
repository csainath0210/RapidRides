Rapid Rides

Whether we’re up for a leisure trip or heading to our next client meeting, sometimes we just need a set of wheels – A Rental Car is the most comfortable way of transport and indeed the best solution to our requirement as its indeed faster than public transport. It offers us the flexibility of choosing cars, meeting our demands at low cost, low maintenance but max comfort and flexibility. The Online Car Rental project that is being undertaken will involve a colorful user interface that is accessible and user-friendly.
The purpose of this project is to develop a system design especially for large, premium and small car rental business. The car rental system provides complete functionality of listing and booking car. The Admin can add a car, manage booked cars, rent cars and also view feedbacks given by the users and respond to enquiries. On the other hand, the User can view the details of available cars, and can easily get a car on rent. The user is also free to give his/ her valuable feedback and inquire in case of queries or clarifications from his/ her side.
